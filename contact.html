Our Story

Ishidoro Zen Gardens Inc. is a newly incorporated company in Calgary, Alberta, Canada. Yet, our roots go a long way back to the 1980s. The founder's father started and managed a small construction company in Puerto La Cruz, Anzoategui, Venezuela. Passion and dedication run in the family. So, the founder relocated to Calgary in the 2000s to build his own world class construction and consulting company, with an aim at providing professional services in whatever combination is required to meet our clients' needs. Our customers are real estate owners who value aesthetics, tranquility, and sustainability in their outdoor spaces. They usually are busy professionals who want a low maintenance garden, retirees who enjoy gardening as a hobby, or families who want a safe and fun place.

You Can Have a Japanese or Zen Styled Garden for Every Season

Contemporary homes do not have great outdoors as land is a highly priced commodity. Does It Matter How Big or Small Your Japanese and Zen Styled Garden Is?

Your Japanese garden is your special place where peace and serenity meet your most basic needs for self expression. Either small or large, for strolling or just for contemplation, Japanese and Zen styled gardens evoke these feelings. According to Shintoism and Buddhism principles, there is not a perfect Japanese garden. Rather, all of them are perfect in essence! So, choose quality over quantity. Check out our suggested landscaping themes. Regardless of what you may find on the web nowadays, Japanese and Zen styled gardens or landscapes have no religious motive. They might be for personal realization or just for fun. But every person has a different drive to accomplish their goals. It has nothing to do with how much wealth one might have or wish to own. Ignore those lousy ads or videos about what you will get with a Japanese garden, and ask yourself: What does this mean to me personally?
